I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Toronto in the group of Prof. Marco Gualtieri. Previously, I was a PhD student at the University of Oxford under the supervision of Prof. Andrew Dancer.

My research is mostly centred around hyperkähler geometry and its interesting connections with Lie theory which arise through gauge theory.

The picture below represents both the set of root subsystems of the exceptional Lie algebra G2 ordered by inclusion and the stratification structure of a singular hyperkähler space constructed from Nahm equations which I studied in this paper.
